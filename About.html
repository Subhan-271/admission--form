About Us

Welcome to Islamabad National University, a place where excellence in education meets a commitment to innovation. Founded in 2025, our university has grown into a prestigious institution known for its rigorous academic programs and vibrant campus life.

Located in the heart of Islamabad, Islamabad National University offers a range of undergraduate designed to prepare students for success in their chosen fields. Our faculty comprises distinguished scholars and industry experts dedicated to providing a supportive and stimulating learning environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a high-quality education that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and ethical leadership. We are committed to preparing our students to become active, informed, and engaged citizens of the world.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Islamabad National University continues to be a leader in higher education, known for its innovative research, global perspective, and dedication to student success.

Why Choose Us?

  • World-class faculty with real-world experience
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
  • Diverse and inclusive campus community
  • Strong connections with industry and alumni

Our Campus

Islamabad National University has several campuses, each offering unique opportunities and specialized programs. Here’s a brief overview:

  • Main Campus: Located in Islamabad, this is the central hub of our university, featuring comprehensive programs across various disciplines.
  • Campus 1: Located in Faisalabad.
  • Campus 2: Located in Lahore.
  • Campus 3: Located in Karachi.
